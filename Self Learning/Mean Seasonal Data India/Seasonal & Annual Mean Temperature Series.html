Proof of rising temperatures(Global warming)¶

The indian subcontinent is experiencing a rise in temperatures inline with the global rising tempratues. Since 1901, the mean annual mean tempratures have risen by 1 degree celsius. We are also experiencing a rise in anuual winter temperatues which means warmer winters and a fall in crop output. If we are not able to curb this trend, the day is not far when there will be a costal tsunamis(due to rise in sea levels) ,food shortage due to fall in the food crop output and a failed future for our coming generations.
